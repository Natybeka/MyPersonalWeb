Why Physics

I believe that my curious nature as perhaps peaked in grade seven which was when my love affair with physics began. I was
very interested in physics because to a certain degree it has answered many questions about our understanding of the universe.
I was so interested in fact that I began to read outside the school curriculum and familiarized myself with the works of Gallileo
and Newton about the heavens' motion, revolutionary work of Albert Eistein's relativity and the contemporary work of Richard
Feynman, a pioneer in the development of quantum theory, because they have challenged the prevailing views about the laws of
nature and succeeded in unvieling some secrets of the universe.

Tech Savy

I have always been fascinated by computers and networks. My dad bought my first computer when I was 10. I was fascinated by

basics of the programming language c++ that I trully began to understand how these fascinating things operated. I found it a refreshing
and neat way of solving the problems of the future.
