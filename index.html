Why Physics

I believe that my curious nature as perhaps peaked in grade seven which was when my love affair with physics began. I was very interested in physics because to a certain degree it has answered many questions about our understanding of the universe. I was so interested in fact that I began to read outside the school curriculum and familiarized myself with the works of Gallileo and Newton about the heavens' motion, revolutionary work of Albert Eistein's relativity and the contemporary work of Richard Feynman, a pioneer in the development of quantum theory, because they have challenged the prevailing views about the laws of nature and succeeded in unvieling some secrets of the universe.
