A List of my Recommendations

...This list is weekly updated to show Recommendations and tips for new things...

1. Books

  • Fantasy fiction
    • A Song of Ice and Fire: This series by George R. R. martin is set in the mystical lands of westeros. The powerful familes Stark, Targaryen, Baratheon, Lannister and Tyrell all fight to have all the power and glory.
    • Harry Potter: Set in a magical world that exists within ours, this books follows the story of a boy who against all odds survives a killing curse by one of the most powerful wizards known. The story follows the growth of Harry and how he deals with many challenges in his life.
    • Artemis Foul: Artemis Foul is an evil genius who is only thirteen. The boy discovers magical creatures and , helped by his servand Butler, come up with nasty plans aimed at his new discovery.
  • Scientific books
    • A brief history of time: written by the brillian physicist Stephen Hawking, he outlines the major discoveries in physics that have revolutionized mankind. He starts from Newton all the way through Einstein and Schrodinger.
    • Guns, germs and Steel: Jared Diamond takes a scientific approach to history as he tries to answer deep questions like why have have of mankind rich and the other hal poor by looking at various geological evidences.
