Cyberpunk 2077, The most anticipated game release in history

By Natnael Bekabtu on March 20, 2020

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification where play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. Althought it is not exactly know how long the game has been in development, it is estimated that it took about 9 years to complete, with the first trailer being released in 2013.

Cyberpunk 2077 is finally arriving in the hands of gamers around the world after over a decade of development. hopefully the game will not have further delays, as it is set to release in April of this year. It is expected to be released for pc first after which it will be released for ps4 and xbox1 consoles.

With the major console looking for a major update at the end of this year with the releases of the Xbox series s and PS5 set for the end of the year, gamers are reluctant about the performance of the game on older platforms like the PS4 and XBox one. There has been a major hint that the will run on only the best machines which is why the developers are releasing the game for the PC (Since it is easy to customize with new video cards and CPUs).

From what is known so far, the game is set in Night City ,an American megacity in the Free State of North California, controlled by corporations and unassailed by the laws of both country and state. It sees conflict from rampant gang wars and its ruling entities contending for dominance. The city is reliant on robotics for everyday aspects like waste collection, maintenance, and public transportation.Its visual identity is derived from the four eras it underwent—austere Entropism, colourful Kitsch, imposing Neo-Militarism, and opulent Neo-Kitsch.Homelessness abounds but does not preclude cybernetic modification for the poor, giving rise to cosmetic addiction and consequent violence. These threats are dealt with by the armed force known as Psycho Squad.Trauma Team can be employed for rapid medical services. Because of the constant threat of physical harm, all citizens are allowed to openly carry firearms in public.
